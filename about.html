About SAKEC E-Cell

Empowering the next generation of entrepreneurs and innovators at Shah & Anchor Kutchhi Engineering College

Our Mission

To foster an entrepreneurial ecosystem within Shah & Anchor Kutchhi Engineering College that encourages innovation, creativity, and sustainable business development among students and faculty.

Our Vision

To be the premier entrepreneurship hub in Mumbai, producing successful entrepreneurs who contribute to economic growth and social development through innovative solutions.

Our Journey

Milestones in our entrepreneurial journey

2019 - Foundation

SAKEC E-Cell was established with a vision to promote entrepreneurship among engineering students. Started with a core team of 15 enthusiastic students.

2020 - First Startup

Incubated our first student startup - a fintech solution that went on to raise ₹50 lakhs in seed funding. This marked the beginning of our success story.

2021 - Recognition

Received the "Best E-Cell" award from Mumbai University for our outstanding contribution to student entrepreneurship and innovation.

2022 - Expansion

Launched our dedicated incubation center with state-of-the-art facilities, mentorship programs, and partnerships with leading VCs.

2023 - Scale Up

Expanded our network to include 50+ mentor partnerships and successfully helped 25 startups raise funding totaling ₹2 crores.

2024 - Innovation Hub

Established our Innovation Hub with advanced prototyping facilities, AI/ML labs, and collaboration spaces for cross-functional teams.
