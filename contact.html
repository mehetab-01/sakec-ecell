Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I join SAKEC E-Cell?

Students can join by attending our recruitment drives held at the beginning of each academic year. We also accept applications throughout the year for specific roles. Follow our social media for announcements.

Do you accept startups from other colleges?

Yes! While our primary focus is on SAKEC students, we welcome innovative startups from other institutions. External applicants go through a competitive selection process.

What kind of support do you provide to startups?

We provide mentorship, workspace, initial funding (up to ₹10 lakhs), legal support, networking opportunities, and access to our alumni network of successful entrepreneurs.

How long is the incubation program?

Our standard incubation program is 6 months, with possibility of extension based on progress and requirements. We also offer pre-incubation programs for early-stage ideas.

Are your events open to external participants?

Most of our events are open to external participants. Some workshops may have limited seats with priority given to SAKEC students. Check individual event details for specific requirements.
