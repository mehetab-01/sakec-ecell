About This Checklist

This comprehensive legal checklist ensures your startup complies with all Indian regulatory requirements. From company incorporation to ongoing compliance, this checklist covers every legal aspect you need to consider.

Developed in collaboration with leading corporate lawyers and verified by legal experts, this checklist has helped over 300 startups avoid legal pitfalls and maintain proper compliance.

Legal Areas Covered:

Company Formation

Registration, MOA, AOA, and initial compliance

Contracts & Agreements

Employment, vendor, customer, and investment contracts

Intellectual Property

Trademarks, patents, copyrights, and trade secrets

Data Protection

Privacy policies, data security, and GDPR compliance

Checklist Includes:

  • Company registration step-by-step guide
  • Required licenses and permits by industry
  • Contract templates and negotiation tips
  • IP protection strategy and filing process
  • Ongoing compliance calendar
  • Legal costs estimation worksheet
