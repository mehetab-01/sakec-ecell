Pitch Deck Best Practices

Expert tips for creating compelling investor presentations

Keep It Concise

Aim for 10-12 slides for the main presentation. Save detailed information for appendix slides. Remember, investors' attention spans are limited.

Use Real Data

Back up your claims with concrete numbers, metrics, and market research. Investors want to see evidence of traction and market validation.

Tell a Story

Create a narrative that connects with investors emotionally. Start with the problem, introduce your solution, and show the opportunity.
