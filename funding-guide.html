Featured Government Schemes

Key funding opportunities for Indian startups

Startup India Seed Fund

Funding Amount: Up to ₹20 Lakhs

Stage: Proof of concept, prototype development

Sector: All sectors

Government initiative to provide financial support to startups for proof of concept, prototype development, product trials, market entry and commercialization.

SIDBI Fund of Funds

Funding Amount: ₹10,000 Cr corpus

Stage: All stages

Sector: All sectors

India's largest fund of funds dedicated to startups, providing funding through registered alternative investment funds (AIFs).

MSME Technology Upgradation

Funding Amount: Up to ₹1 Cr

Stage: Technology implementation

Sector: Manufacturing, technology

Scheme to facilitate technology upgradation in MSME sector by providing capital subsidy for technology upgradation.
